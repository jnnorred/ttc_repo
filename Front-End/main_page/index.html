About Title Town Catering


Est.2021

We are newly founded Catering company based out of Tuscaloosa, Alabama. As a family business and local grown we strive for quality in providing for our community. We cater to any family or event, no matter how big or small

TTC prioritizes the satisfaction of our clients, and we do whatever we can to ensure that your event, whether it’s your wedding, a corporate lunch, or a private event, is hassle-free and has great food that reflects your preferences and tastes. We go to great lengths to ensure that the least of yours and your guests' worries is incredible food with beautiful presentation — that is guaranteed! We are happy to provide unmatched catering services to Tuscaloosa, Alabama and the surrounding areas. Contact us to learn more.
